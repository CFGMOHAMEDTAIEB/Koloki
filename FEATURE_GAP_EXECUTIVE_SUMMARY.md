## 🔴 THE 5 CRITICAL BLOCKERS

**App CANNOT Launch Without These:**

### 1. **BOOKING SYSTEM** (0% complete)
- ❌ No property calendar
- ❌ No booking flow (request → payment → confirmation)
- ❌ No date selection
- ❌ No check-in/check-out process
- **Impact**: Renters can message owners, but can't BOOK anything
- **User Impact**: "How do I actually book this place?"

### 2. **ESCROW PAYMENT SYSTEM** (50% complete)
- ❌ No payment holding until check-in
- ❌ Money goes directly to owner = scam risk
- ❌ No dispute resolution before payment released
- ❌ No 48-hour dispute window
- **Impact**: Platform liable for fraud/scams
- **Legal Risk**: 🔴 Could face lawsuits

### 3. **IDENTITY VERIFICATION (KYC)** (15% complete)
- ❌ No government ID verification
- ❌ No email confirmation
- ❌ No phone verification
- ❌ No background checks
- **Impact**: Scammers run rampant
- **User Experience**: Renters don't trust unknown owners

### 4. **REVIEW & RATING SYSTEM** (0% complete)
- ❌ No review prompts after booking
- ❌ No 1-5 star ratings
- ❌ No review verification
- ❌ No owner response capability
- **Impact**: No trust signals for new listings
- **Competitive Gap**: Airbnb has 100M+ reviews

### 5. **LEGAL DOCUMENTS** (10% complete)
- ❌ No Terms & Conditions
- ❌ No Privacy Policy (GDPR required)
- ❌ No house rules
- ❌ No liability disclaimers
- **Impact**: Regulatory non-compliance
- **Legal Risk**: 🔴 Could be fined/shut down
