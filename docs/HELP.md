# Help Center

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?

1. Download the Coloki app or visit our website
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email, phone number, and password
4. Verify your email and phone number
5. Complete your profile information
6. Start browsing or creating listings

### What's the difference between a Renter and Owner account?

- **Renter Account**: Browse properties, favorite listings, contact owners, and apply for colocations
- **Owner Account**: Create property listings, manage bookings, communicate with renters, and receive payments

### How do I verify my identity?

1. Go to Settings > Verification
2. Click "Start KYC Verification"
3. Upload a clear photo of your ID (passport or driver's license)
4. Verify your information matches your ID
5. Wait for Coloki approval (usually 24-48 hours)

## For Renters

### How do I search for properties?

1. Use the map or list view to browse properties
2. Filter by location, price, room type, and amenities
3. Click on a property to view details
4. Check reviews and ratings from previous renters

### How do I apply for a colocation?

1. Find a property you like
2. Click "Send Application" or "Contact Owner"
3. Write a brief message about yourself
4. Submit your application
5. Wait for owner's response

### What are the fees for renters?

- **NO upfront fees for renters**
- You only pay rent and utilities as agreed with the owner
- All negotiations are between you and the owner

### What if I have issues with the property or owner?

1. Try to resolve directly with the owner
2. If unresolved, contact Coloki support
3. We can mediate disputes and facilitate resolution
4. Escalation to arbitration if necessary

## For House Owners

### How do I post a property?

1. Go to Dashboard > New Listing
2. Fill in property details (title, description, location)
3. Upload 5-10 high-quality photos
4. Select room types and prices
5. Add amenities and house rules
6. Pay the 10 DT listing fee
7. Property will be verified within 24 hours

### What information should I include in my listing?

- Clear, honest property description
- Current, high-quality photos
- Accurate location pin
- Available room types and prices
- Amenities (WiFi, parking, laundry, etc.)
- House rules and tenant preferences
- Preferred lease duration

### How do I get paid?

1. When a colocation is confirmed, Coloki charges 10% commission
2. The remaining 90% goes to your wallet
3. You can withdraw funds to your bank account
4. Withdrawal requests are processed within 3-5 business days

### I posted a property but it's not appearing. Why?

Property listings go through verification:
- Profile must be KYC verified
- Listing details must be complete and accurate
- Photos must clearly show the property
- No policy violations
- Typical verification time: 24 hours

If your listing is rejected, you'll receive a notification with the reason. Contact support if you need help resubmitting.

### What's the 10 DT listing fee for?

The listing fee helps:
- Combat spam and false listings
- Maintain platform quality
- Fund verification processes
- Support customer service
- Keep platform secure

## Payments & Refunds

### How do Coloki payments work?

- Listing fee (10 DT): One-time, non-refundable
- Commission (10%): Charged when colocation is confirmed
- All payments processed through Stripe (secure)

### Can I get a refund?

- **Listing Fee**: Non-refundable (it's used to verify your property)
- **Commission**: Non-refundable for confirmed transactions
- **Disputed Charges**: Contact support within 30 days

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security:
- PCI DSS compliant payment processing
- Stripe handles all payment data
- Coloki never stores full credit card numbers
- SSL/TLS encryption

## Account & Security

### How do I change my password?

1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm changes

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login screen
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click link and create new password
5. Log in with new password

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes safely

### I think my account was hacked. What should I do?

1. **Immediately**: Change your password
2. **Then**: Enable 2FA
3. **Contact us**: Email security@coloki.com with details
4. **Review**: Check recent login activity in Settings
5. **Monitor**: Watch your wallet and transactions

## Disputes & Safety

### What if I have a dispute with an owner/renter?

1. Try to resolve directly
2. Use in-app messaging for documentation
3. Contact Coloki support with details
4. We'll mediate and investigate
5. Escalate to arbitration if needed

### Is Coloki responsible for disputes?

- We facilitate resolution but are not parties to rental agreements
- User-to-user disputes are primarily their responsibility
- We intervene when Terms are violated

### How do I report unsafe behavior?

1. Click the user's profile
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose the reason (violence, harassment, scam, etc.)
4. Provide details and evidence
5. Submit report

Coloki investigates all reports and takes appropriate action, including account suspension or ban.

## Technical Issues

### The app keeps crashing. What should I do?

1. Update to latest app version
2. Clear app cache: Settings > Storage > Clear Cache
3. Restart your device
4. Reinstall the app if issue persists
5. Contact support: support@coloki.com

### I can't upload photos. How do I fix this?

1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure sufficient storage space
3. Try using WiFi instead of mobile data
4. Resize photos before upload (max 10MB each)
5. Try a different browser or clear cache

### I'm not receiving notifications

1. Check app notifications are enabled in Settings
2. Check device notification settings
3. Email notifications: Verify in email preferences
4. Check spam/junk folder for emails
5. Contact support if still not working
