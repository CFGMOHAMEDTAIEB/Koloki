# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 30, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Coloki ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform, including our mobile application and website.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Name, email address, phone number
- Identity verification documents (ID, passport)
- Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
- Property details and images
- Messages and communications with other users
- Profile information and preferences
- Location data (when you allow it)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Device information (device type, OS, browser)
- IP address and location
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent, interactions)
- Analytics data

### 2.3 Third-Party Information
- We may receive information from background check services
- Information from payment processors
- Publicly available information from social media profiles

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- Creating and maintaining your account
- Processing payments and commissions
- Verifying identity and conducting KYC procedures
- Personalizing your experience
- Improving our platform and services
- Sending notifications and communications
- Detecting and preventing fraud
- Complying with legal obligations
- Conducting background checks (with your consent)
- Analytics and platform optimization

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- PCI DSS compliance for payment data
- Limited access to personal information

### 4.2 Limitations
No security system is impenetrable. While we implement strong security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Share Data With:
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe (payment processing)
- **Verification Services**: Third-party background check providers
- **Legal Authorities**: When required by law or to prevent harm
- **Service Providers**: Email, hosting, and analytics providers
- **Other Users**: Limited information (name, ratings, reviews) for facilitation purposes

### 5.2 We Do NOT Share:
- Sensitive identification documents with other users
- Payment information with other users
- Personal data for marketing purposes without consent

## 6. Your Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

### 6.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request a copy of your personal data.

### 6.2 Right to Correction
You can request correction of inaccurate data.

### 6.3 Right to Deletion
You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a portable format.

### 6.5 Right to Opt-Out
You can opt-out of non-essential communications and data processing.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

- We use cookies to enhance user experience
- You can disable cookies in your browser settings
- This may limit functionality of the Platform

## 8. Data Retention

- User account data: Retained for the duration of your account + 3 years
- Transaction records: Retained for 7 years (accounting compliance)
- Identification documents: Retained for 3 years after account closure
- Communication logs: Retained for 2 years

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## 10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies.

## 11. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes become effective when posted. Your continued use constitutes acceptance.
