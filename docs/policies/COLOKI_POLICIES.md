# Coloki Platform Policies

## User Protection Policies

### 1. Identity Verification (KYC)

All users must complete Know-Your-Customer (KYC) verification:
- Valid government-issued ID required
- Photos and information must match ID
- Verification completed within 24-48 hours
- Failed verification users get 2 attempts
- Continued non-verification results in account suspension

**Benefits of Verification:**
- Increased trust among community members
- Access to all platform features
- Better protection against fraud
- Higher conversion rates for renters/owners

### 2. Background Checks

House owners must consent to background checks:
- Checks include criminal history search
- Conducted by trusted third-party providers
- Results kept confidential (pass/fail only)
- Check results valid for 2 years
- Renewal required after expiration

### 3. Property Verification

All properties undergo verification:
- Actual property viewing verification (digital audit)
- Information accuracy check
- Safety standards compliance
- Regulation compliance check
- Document verification

**Verification Status:**
- ✅ **Verified**: Property meets all requirements
- ⏳ **Pending**: Under review
- ❌ **Rejected**: Violation of policy (notification provided)

### 4. User Ratings & Reviews

- Users can rate transactions 1-5 stars
- Written reviews encouraged but optional
- Reviews must be factual and respectful
- Fake or malicious reviews are removed
- Users can respond to reviews
- Rating impacts user trustworthiness score

## Safety Policies

### 1. Communication Guidelines

- All initial contact should occur within app
- Do NOT exchange personal numbers initially
- Avoid meeting in private locations for initial contact
- Use a public meeting place to view property
- Bring a friend or family member

### 2. Financial Safety

- **Do NOT transfer money outside platform**
- Use only Coloki's secure payment system
- Be cautious of owners requesting upfront payments
- Report any unusual financial requests
- All payments have transaction records

### 3. Property Safety

Owners are required to:
- Install functional locks on all doors
- Maintain smoke detectors
- Provide emergency contact information
- Maintain property cleanliness
- Report maintenance issues promptly

Renters agree to:
- Respect safety equipment
- Report maintenance needs
- Follow emergency procedures
- Not tamper with security devices

### 4. Anti-Discrimination Policy

Coloki strictly prohibits discrimination based on:
- Race or ethnicity
- Religion or beliefs
- Gender or gender identity
- Sexual orientation
- Age (except minimum age requirements)
- Disability
- National origin

**Violations Result In:**
- First offense: Warning
- Second offense: Account suspension (30-90 days)
- Third offense: Permanent ban

### 5. Sexual Harassment & Abuse Policy

Any form of sexual harassment or abuse will result in:
- Immediate account termination
- Reporting to local authorities
- Blacklist from platform
- Cooperation with law enforcement

## Financial Policies

### 1. Commission Structure

- **10% Commission** on all confirmed colocations
- Charged to property owner
- Non-refundable for completed transactions
- Transparent breakdown provided

### 2. Listing Fees

- **10 DT per property listing**
- Non-refundable (covers verification costs)
- One-time payment per property
- Required before property activation
- Payment via Stripe

### 3. Wallet & Withdrawals

- Monthly wallet balance reports provided
- Minimum withdrawal: 50 DT
- Maximum withdrawal: 10,000 DT per transaction
- Withdrawal fee: 1.5% or 1 DT (whichever is greater)
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

### 4. Dispute & Refunds

- Commission disputes must be reported within 30 days
- Listing fees are never refunded
- Payment disputes handled through Stripe
- Fraudulent refund requests result in account suspension

## Data Privacy Policies

### 1. Data Collection

All data collection follows GDPR and CCPA standards:
- Users have right to access their data
- Users can request data deletion
- Users can export their data
- Privacy policy regularly updated
- Opt-out options for non-essential processing

### 2. Data Sharing

Data shared only with:
- Payment processors (Stripe)
- Verification services
- Legal authorities (if required)
- Service providers (secure agreements)
- NOT shared for marketing without consent

### 3. Data Retention

- Account data: 3 years after account closure
- Transactions: 7 years (legal compliance)
- Identification docs: 3 years after use
- Messages: 2 years
- Deleted data purged from backups: 1 year

## Content Policies

### 1. Prohibited Content

Not allowed on platform:
- Illegal activities or services
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Graphic violence or adult content
- Phishing or scam content
- Copyrighted material without permission
- Personal information of others
- Spam or excessive advertising

### 2. Property Listings

Must not include:
- False or misleading information
- Rooms that don't meet safety standards
- Illegal rental situations
- Discrimination in tenant selection
- Multiple price quotes for same room

### 3. User-Generated Content

Users are responsible for:
- Accuracy of information
- Legality of content
- Respecting others' rights
- Proper usage of images
- Honest reviews and ratings

## Grievance & Arbitration Policies

### 1. Grievance Procedure

Step 1: Direct Communication
- Contact other party through app
- Allow 48 hours for response
- Document all communications

Step 2: Coloki Mediation
- Submit formal complaint to support@coloki.com
- Include documentation and evidence
- Coloki mediator assigned within 24 hours
- Mediation session within 5 days

Step 3: Escalation
- If mediation fails, escalate to arbitration
- Binding arbitration conducted by neutral party
- Both parties must participate in good faith

### 2. Resolution Authority

- Coloki has authority to enforce policies
- Decisions are binding
- Limited appeal process available
- Arbitration costs split equally (unless policy violation clear)

## Advertising Policies

### 1. Advertiser Requirements

- Must be verified users with valid identification
- No prohibited products/services
- Ads must be factually accurate
- Compliance with local advertising laws
- Transparent pricing for ad placements

### 2. Ad Placement & Display

- Ads clearly marked as "Sponsored"
- CPM (Cost Per Mille) pricing transparent
- Impression and click tracking provided
- Publishers can pause ads anytime
- Monthly performance reports

### 3. Prohibited Advertising

Not allowed:
- Illegal products or services
- Misleading health/medicine claims
- Phishing or scam-like content
- Adult content
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Copyrighted material

## Enforcement & Penalties

### Violation Scale

**Minor Violations:**
- Warning + content removal
- Examples: Typos in listings, low-quality photos

**Moderate Violations:**
- 7-30 day account suspension
- Examples: Misleading information, first-time harassment

**Major Violations:**
- 30-90 day suspension or permanent ban
- Examples: Fraud, repeated discrimination, safety violations

**Critical Violations:**
- Immediate permanent ban + law enforcement reporting
- Examples: Violence, sexual abuse, major financial fraud

### Appeal Process

- Appeals must be filed within 14 days
- Submit evidence supporting your case
- Manual review by policy team
- Response within 7-10 days
- Limited to one appeal per violation

## Policy Updates

Policies are reviewed quarterly and updated as needed:
- Users notified 30 days before major changes
- Continued use implies acceptance
- Old policies archived on our website
- Impact assessment provided for significant changes
